Introduction

I have young children just like you. Recently, my daughter came home from Kindergarten with a list of words she was expected to know by sight. That was the moment I had the idea for SWIRL. There are other apps that provide simple flash cards but your child needs more. Your child needs to develop a love of stories. This comes from reading with your child. SWIRL combines Sight Words with the joy of discovering classics such as The Wizard of Oz, The Velveteen Rabbit and other beautifully written books loved by generations.

Please report any issues you have so I can fix what's important to you as soon as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Sightwords?

Sight Words are words that should be recognised instantly by your child. Your child should be able to read these words without having to sound them out. There are many Sight Word systems, Dolch is just one. The original Dolch Sight Words were chosen based on their frequency in common childrens books. Starting with 220 words divided into 5 grade levels, a further 95 nouns were added to the list.

What does SWIRL stand for?

  • Sight
  • Words
  • Interactive
  • Reading
  • Lists

How can my child get the most out of SWIRL?

  1. Choose a classic children's book
  2. Choose a word list
  3. Start Reading to your child
  4. Tap a sight word marked in red
  5. Help your child complete the activities, now the word is marked in blue
  6. Let your child read all the words marked in blue
  7. Choose a new list for your child's favourite book

Why the Humpty Dumpty character?

The very first protoype for SWIRL used a story from the 18th century about the son of Humpty Dumpty. Humpty Dumpty seemed a perfect fit and he will continue to be the face of SWIRL.
